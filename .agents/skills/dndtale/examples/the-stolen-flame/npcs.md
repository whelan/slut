# NPCs - The Stolen Flame

---

## Major NPCs

### Elder Mira Thorne

**Role:** Village leader and quest giver
**Race/Gender:** Female Human
**Location:** Redbrook Village
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Scene 1

#### Appearance
A weather-worn woman in her sixties with silver-streaked black hair tied in a practical bun. She wears simple but well-maintained clothing and carries herself with quiet authority despite the worry lines on her face.

#### Personality
**Traits:** Practical, protective, stoic
**Ideals:** Community above self
**Bonds:** Redbrook is her life's work
**Flaws:** Slow to trust outsiders

#### Mannerisms & Speech
- Crosses her arms when thinking
- Direct speech, wastes no words
- Addresses party as "friends" if they help, "travelers" if neutral

#### Background & History
Mira has been Elder of Redbrook for twenty years. Her family has protected this village for three generations. The theft of the Eternal Flame happened on her watch, and she feels personally responsible.

#### Motivations & Goals
**Wants:** The Eternal Flame returned immediately
**Needs:** To prove she can still protect her village
**Fears:** The village will die without the Flame's protection

#### Secrets
Her daughter left Redbrook years ago to become an adventurer and never returned. Mira hopes these adventurers can succeed where her daughter may have failed.

#### Relationships
- **Brother Kellan:** Trusts him completely, he's her spiritual advisor
- **The Party:** Initially wary but grateful for their help

#### Stat Block
**Stats:** Noble (MM p.348)
**Modifications:** Replace rapier with quarterstaff

#### Roleplaying Tips
- She's desperate but tries not to show it
- Direct and matter-of-fact about the danger
- Softens if party shows genuine care for the village
- Offers family heirloom weapon if truly impressed

### Sleetscale

**Role:** Antagonist (Young White Dragon)
**Race/Gender:** Dragon
**Location:** Dragon's Lair
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Scene 5

#### Appearance
A young white dragon, barely larger than a horse, with gleaming scales like fresh snow and piercing blue eyes. She's beautiful but clearly vain, preening and posturing constantly.

#### Personality
**Traits:** Arrogant, greedy, surprisingly reasonable
**Ideals:** Deserves the best of everything
**Bonds:** Her growing hoard
**Flaws:** Young and inexperienced, can be outsmarted

#### Mannerisms & Speech
- Hisses when annoyed
- Refers to herself in third person ("Sleetscale demands...")
- Easily flattered

#### Background & History
Sleetscale left her mother's lair only a season ago. She claimed this cave and the kobolds that live here, but she's still learning how to be a proper dragon. The Eternal Flame's warmth is comforting in her cold lair.

#### Motivations & Goals
**Wants:** A warm, comfortable lair worthy of a dragon
**Needs:** Validation that she's a "real" dragon
**Fears:** Being seen as weak or small

#### Secrets
She's terrified her mother will find out she's not doing well. She barely controls the kobolds and is in over her head.

#### Relationships
- **Kreeg (kobold leader):** Her "loyal" minion (he's just afraid of her)
- **Her mother:** Wants to impress her, fears disappointing her
- **The Party:** Sees them as either toys or threats

#### Stat Block
**Stats:** **White Dragon Wyrmling** (MM p.102)

#### Roleplaying Tips
- She's arrogant but can be reasoned with
- Values treasure, comfort, and recognition
- Would rather make a deal than die
- Speaks in grandiose terms but has childish tantrums

#### Story Role
**Acts as:** Primary antagonist
**Can Provide:** The Eternal Flame (if convinced)
**Will Ask For:** Tribute, recognition, alternative heat source

#### Potential Outcomes
- **If befriended:** Could become recurring ally
- **If antagonized:** Flees when bloodied, swears revenge
- **If killed:** Kobolds scatter, possible mother dragon revenge later

## Supporting NPCs

### Brother Kellan

**Role:** Temple priest who guarded the Flame
**Race/Gender:** Male Human
**Location:** Redbrook Temple
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Scene 1

#### Quick Description
Middle-aged priest with thinning brown hair and kind eyes, wearing simple robes with sun-god symbols.

#### Personality
Gentle and guilt-ridden. Blames himself for not preventing the theft.
**Quirk:** Constantly apologizes

#### What He Knows
- The theft happened three nights ago around midnight
- He heard kobold voices ("yipping and chittering")
- They left footprints heading northeast toward the hills
- The Eternal Flame must stay lit or the village's protection fades

#### What He Wants
Redemption and the Flame's return

#### Stat Block
**Stats:** Priest (MM p.348)

#### Roleplaying Notes
Speak softly, apologize frequently, very grateful to the party

### Kreeg

**Role:** Kobold leader in dragon's lair
**Race/Gender:** Male Kobold
**Location:** Dragon's Lair
**First Appearance:** Chapter 1, Scene 4 (or earlier if captured)

#### Quick Description
Slightly larger kobold with a crude bone necklace and a rusty short sword.

#### Personality
Cowardly but cunning. Loyal to Sleetscale out of fear, not devotion.
**Quirk:** Ends sentences with "yes-yes!"

#### What He Knows
- Sleetscale ordered the Flame stolen
- The dragon "wants warm-warm, yes-yes!"
- There are 12 kobolds total in the lair
- The dragon's chamber is through the water pools

#### What He Wants
To survive

#### Stat Block
**Stats:** Kobold (MM p.195)
**Modifications:** +2 HP, 14 AC (leather armor)

#### Roleplaying Notes
High-pitched voice, refers to dragon as "Great Sleetscale," will betray dragon to save his own life
