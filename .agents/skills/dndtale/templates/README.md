## Character Concept Guidance

### What Works Well

[Suggest character concepts that fit the campaign. Examples:]

- **Heroic Defenders:** Characters who protect the innocent
- **Treasure Seekers:** Adventurers looking for fame and fortune
- **Mystery Solvers:** Investigators and scholars
- **[Other concepts]**

### Character Motivations

Your character should have a reason to:
1. **Adventure:** Why do they leave safety and risk their life?
2. **Cooperate:** Why do they work with a group rather than alone?
3. **Care:** What do they care about in the world?

Consider giving your character:
- A **goal** (something they want to achieve)
- A **fear** (something they want to avoid)
- A **secret** (something they're hiding)
- A **connection** (someone they care about)

### Connections to the World

[How can players tie their characters to the setting? Provide hooks:]

- Are you from [major city]? If so, what district?
- Do you belong to any of these factions? [List factions players can know about]
- Do you know any of these NPCs? [List NPCs players might reasonably know]

### Party Cohesion

**Important:** This campaign works best when the party works together. Consider how your character connects to the other PCs. You can:
- Have existing relationships (friends, rivals, family, colleagues)
- Share a common goal
- Have complementary skills and interests

**During Session Zero**, we'll make sure everyone's characters have reasons to adventure together.
