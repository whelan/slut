### Modifying NPC Stats

To make a generic NPC more powerful or unique:
- Increase hit points (use maximum instead of average)
- Increase AC (better armor or natural armor)
- Add a magic item or special ability
- Add class levels or proficiencies

To make an NPC weaker:
- Reduce hit points
- Lower AC
- Remove special abilities
- Reduce ability scores
