Top-down battlemap of an enormous open bone plaza at the foot of the Temple of
Tiamat. The plaza is 250 ft long and 100 ft wide, paved with cracked obsidian
flagstones and littered with massive dragon bones — ribs, vertebrae, a
centerpiece dragon skull as large as a wagon staring blindly toward the temple.

On one side: two tunnel mouths emerging from black volcanic rock cliffs, each
guarded by stone archways carved into draconic jaws. Cult banners hang nearby.

On the opposite side: the temple's front entrance — a massive bone-and-stone
archway flanked by twisting spire bases. The doorway is dark, ritualistic.
Glimpse of blue chapel light spilling from the entrance.

Surrounding the plaza: jagged volcanic walls of the caldera. Scattered
sacrifice altars (small, currently empty) along the edges. Patches of dried
blood and broken weaponry from past skirmishes. Faint chromatic glow seeping
through cracks in the temple's outer walls.

Open ground in the middle (clear of obstacles) for the crossing skill challenge.
A few large bone piles provide cover for stealthy approach.

Topdown perspective, no grid, no text, no labels, no UI elements, no character
tokens. Dark fantasy battlemap, painterly rendering, dramatic dusk lighting
(red ritual glow + fading sunset), foundry-VTT-ready, ultra detailed.
