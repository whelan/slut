Full-body portrait of Veksin Modar, the youngest of Rath Modar's apprentices —
a young human male in his early twenties, barely more than a boy. Recently
shaven head with a few patches of stubble missed by the razor, suggesting
inexperience. Soft, undefined features still carrying baby-fat. Worried, naive
brown eyes.

He wears patched, ill-fitting Red Wizard robes that are too large for him,
clearly handed down. The crimson cloth is faded and stained at the cuffs. A
simple wooden ritual focus hangs around his neck on a leather thong, beside a
small brass ring engraved with his mother's name.

Posture: standing in mid-chant, eyes closed, hands raised in ritual gesture,
hands trembling slightly. He looks more like a frightened scholar than a
cultist. A subtle, sad detail: his lips move in humming a lullaby rhythm rather
than the proper chant.

Background hint: faint blue chapel light, a stone altar barely visible behind
him, dissolving into shadow.
