Strategic overview map of the Well of Dragons — an extinct volcanic caldera in
the Sunset Mountains. Massive circular basin of black volcanic rock surrounded
by sheer cliffs. Ground covered in ash and littered with enormous dragon bones
— ribs the size of cathedral arches, skulls bigger than carts.

At the center of the caldera: the Temple of Tiamat, a sinister structure
piercing upward through dragon bones, with five chromatic spires (black, blue,
green, red, white) and a dome that pulses with five-colored ritual light.

Surrounding the temple: an open bone plaza (250 ft × 100 ft) clear of obstacles.
Cult tunnels and lava tube entrances ring the caldera edge, marked with cultist
banners. Lava channels (mostly cooled) trace the volcanic walls.

Above: chromatic dragons circling in formation through black ash clouds, with
distant metallic dragon shapes engaging them at the periphery.

Below the rim: allied army positions visible — red banners (Lords' Alliance),
green (Emerald Enclave), grey (Order of the Gauntlet), orange (Flaming Fist),
blue (Dwarven sappers) — pressing in from multiple approaches.

Top-down map perspective, no grid, no text, no labels, no UI elements. Sword
Coast strategic war-map style, painted on aged parchment with subtle texture.
Dramatic atmospheric lighting from ritual glow at center. Ultra detailed,
foundry-VTT-ready.
