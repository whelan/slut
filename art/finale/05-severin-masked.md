Full-body portrait of Severin Silrajin, supreme leader of the Cult of the
Dragon and final boss of Tyranny of Dragons. Tall, slender, charismatic male
human in his late thirties. He wears the complete Mask of the Dragon Queen — an
elaborate metal mask with five chromatic dragon faces (black, blue, green, red,
white) interwoven into a single nightmare visage, each dragon's eyes glowing
with corresponding elemental light.

He floats slightly above the ground, robes billowing without wind. Layered
robes of deep purple and obsidian-black, edged with gold dragon scales, sleeves
falling past his hands. Around him swirl five smaller orbiting masks (one for
each chromatic dragon), each held aloft by invisible force.

Posture: arms raised slightly, palms outward, in mid-ritual. Composed,
contemptuous, certain of victory. Behind him hints of a swirling chromatic
portal with draconic eyes pressing through.

Token-style composition: full body, transparent background, high contrast
silhouette suitable for VTT use. Dark fantasy, painterly, cinematic gothic
lighting, ultra detailed, no text, no watermark, no UI elements.
