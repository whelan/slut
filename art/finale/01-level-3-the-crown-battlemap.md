Top-down battlemap of an enormous circular ritual chamber: The Crown of Tiamat,
final summit of the Temple of Tiamat. A central raised dais of black obsidian
veined with five colored ritual channels (red, blue, green, black, white) that
spiral inward toward a swirling kaleidoscopic portal at the very center. The
portal pulses with chromatic energy and hints of five draconic eyes pressing
through from beyond.

Around the dais: five smaller platforms positioned at compass points, each
marked with a colored sigil (chromatic dragon symbols). Connecting the dais to
the platforms: narrow bone bridges with no railings, suspended over a vast
abyss filled with cultist sacrificial pits glowing dimly far below.

The outer ring shows the upper walls of the temple — five massive carved dragon
skulls, jaws open, each spire-tower different color. Ash-grey stone floor with
cracks bleeding faint chromatic light. Broken pillars carved with draconic
runes. Cultist robes and abandoned ritual implements scattered near the edges.

Topdown perspective, no grid, no text, no labels, no UI elements, no character
tokens. Atmospheric dark fantasy battlemap, painterly rendering, dramatic
shadows, ritual light glow, foundry-VTT-ready, ultra detailed, high contrast.
