Cinematic scene: Tiamat partially manifesting through a kaleidoscopic portal
above the ritual dais of the Crown of Tiamat. Five enormous draconic heads
press through a swirling vortex of chromatic light — black, blue, green, red,
and white — each head as large as a small castle. Each head is distinctly
different: the black head sneers with acid dripping; the blue head crackles
with controlled lightning; the green head whispers with poison-mist tongue
between dripping fangs; the red head roars with fire in its throat; the white
head opens its jaws in primal hunger.

The five necks twist together at the vortex's center, where reality bends and
tears. Tiamat herself is not fully through — only the heads are visible,
emerging from the portal as if from another dimension. Behind/around her, hints
of Avernus's infernal sky: red-black storm clouds, fire rain, distant
mountains of black slag.

In the foreground (silhouetted, small for scale): the ritual dais with five
colored channels spiraling into the portal. A tiny figure (Severin) floating
beneath the portal, arms raised.

The entire scene radiates apocalyptic dread — the chromatic energies have torn
the temple ceiling open. Stars and shadows visible above through the breach.

Painterly, cinematic, dramatic chiaroscuro lighting, dark fantasy splash art,
ultra detailed, no text, no watermark, no UI. Reminiscent of classic D&D
campaign cover art depicting Tiamat's emergence.
