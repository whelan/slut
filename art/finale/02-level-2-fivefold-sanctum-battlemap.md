Top-down battlemap of the Fivefold Sanctum — middle level of the Temple of
Tiamat. Five distinct platforms arranged in a pentagonal pattern around a
central open void, connected by narrow stone bridges with dragon-bone railings.

Each platform has a unique environmental theme:
- North platform: Black acid basin, bubbling pool of dark liquid surrounded by
  dripping stone and skeletal remains
- Northeast platform: Blue lightning spire, towering crystal pillar crackling
  with arc-light, ozone shimmer
- Southeast platform: Green poison mist garden, twisted vegetation choked in
  pale green vapor and hanging vines
- Southwest platform: Red ember altar, glowing magma cracks beneath a fire-pit
  altar with chains and burnt offerings
- Northwest platform: White frost fang dais, frozen claw-shaped pillars rising
  from a circle of crystalline ice

Central void: a black pit gazing down into Level 1 below, glimpses of the bone
plaza visible through gaps in the floor.

Stone bridges between platforms are narrow (10 ft wide), broken in places,
hazardous. Dragonbone arches frame the inter-platform spaces.

Topdown perspective, no grid, no text, no labels, no UI, no character tokens.
Dark fantasy battlemap, painterly, dramatic chromatic lighting from each focus,
foundry-VTT-ready, ultra detailed, high contrast painterly rendering.
