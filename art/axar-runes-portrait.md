A dwarven wizard of the Evocation school, level 15 veteran of the Tyranny of
Dragons campaign. Iron-grey beard braided with three brass rings, etched with
small runic clasps. He wears layered traveling robes of charcoal and oxblood,
embroidered with faintly glowing arcane sigils. In one hand he grips a polished
obsidian focus; pale blue evocation light flickers between his fingertips,
catching dust motes in the air. Confident, weathered face — a strategist's eyes.
Three-quarter angle, half-portrait composition. Dramatic side lighting from the
left, deep shadows on the right. Background dissolves into smoky darkness with
a hint of dragon-shaped silhouettes implied rather than shown.
