# Level 1 Adaptation Guide: Black Chapel Entry

**File:** `niveau-1-the-maw-BLACK-CHAPEL-ADAPT.md`

**Purpose:** Replaces the original Gate of Bone encounter when the party enters the Temple of Tiamat via the lava tube into **area 5 (Black Chapel)** instead of the main gate.

---

## What Changed

| Element | Original (Gate of Bone) | New (Black Chapel) |
|---|---|---|
| **Entry location** | Area 1 (Blue Chapel / main gate) | Area 5 (Black Chapel / lava tube exit) |
| **Encounter A enemy** | White Abishai + Dragonfang + Dragonwings + Dragonclaws | Single Red Wizard (Mage, CR 6) |
| **Surprise round** | No surprise | Party has surprise if stealthy |
| **Reinforcements** | None; combat is local | 4 other Red Wizards arrive R2+ from distant chapels |
| **Portal collapse** | Not possible | Possible if <5 Wizards perform ritual for 2 consecutive rounds |
| **Clock penalty for quick win** | N/A | –2 if portal collapses (largest single reversal) |
| **Fen's location** | In the Procession | Marching in Procession (visible from Black Chapel floor) |
| **Prisoner sacrifice** | Not visible | Visible as sound during Procession scene; bodies later at apse |

---

## How to Use This Adaptation

### Before the Session

1. Read `niveau-1-the-maw-BLACK-CHAPEL-ADAPT.md` in full.
2. Note the **Red Wizard stat block** (uses standard Mage, CR 6).
3. Prepare the **three clock outcomes** table (section "Three Possible Clock Outcomes").
4. Mark the **Procession boxed text** (Danish) for easy reference.

### During Play

1. **Party emerges from lava tube → area 5 (Black Chapel).**
   - Read "The Arrival" boxed text.
   - Roll initiative.
   - If party is stealthy, they get a surprise round. Mage acts last in R1.

2. **Encounter A – The Black Chapel Red Wizard.**
   - Mage's tactics are detailed round-by-round.
   - Watch for Counterspell vs. Axar.
   - The **immediate victory condition** (portal collapse) is possible if party is efficient.

3. **Encounter B – The Procession.**
   - During R2–3 of combat (or immediately after), the party hears the procession.
   - Four options: let them pass, stop via combat, Persuasion, or Sleight of Hand.
   - This is where **Fen recognition** happens (Daxx Insight DC 13).
   - **Frygtløs parallel beat:** He can choose to save Fen or let him go. No judgment either way.

4. **Calculate clock outcome** using the three-outcome table.
   - Best (–3): All three encounters resolved optimally.
   - Medium (0): Mixed results.
   - Worst (+2): Red Wizard survives, procession fully passes.

5. **Transition to Level 2** using the appropriate boxed text (varies by clock outcome).

## Clock Outcomes Summary

| Scenario | Clock | Narrative |
|---|---|---|
| Kill R1 mage + block ritual 2 rounds + save/stop procession | –3 or –2 | Portal flickers. Ritual severely interrupted. Severin sounds shaken. |
| Kill R1 mage, standard procession handling | 0 | Portal stabilizes. Ritual recovers. Normal entry into Level 2. |
| Mage survives to R2+, procession fully passes | +2 | Portal brightens. Four mages now engaged. Ritual accelerates. |

## Key Differences from Original

**What's gone:**
- Gate of Bone (bone barricades, White Abishai sniper).
- Dragonfang/Dragonwing/Dragonclaw unit.
- Infiltration skill challenge.

**What's new:**
- Single Red Wizard can be dispatched quickly if party is efficient.
- **Surprise round advantage** rewards stealth and scouting.
- **Portal collapse mechanic** creates a unique victory condition (–2 clock immediately).
- **Fen relocation** makes him part of the Procession scene, not separate.
- **Clock outcomes are more granular**: –3 possible (best), +2 possible (worst).

---

## Important Notes

1. **Ritual continuation:** 5 Red Wizards must perform the ritual each round for it to progress. If fewer than 5 do so for 2 consecutive rounds, the portal collapses and clock resets to 0 (major reversal).

2. **Frygtløs parallel:** The Naergoth moment (he swung first) is meant to be mirrored here with Fen. Do NOT explain. Let the player discover the parallel through play.

3. **Fen's state depends on party choice:**
   - Let procession pass: Dead at apse. Used in aftermath narration only.
   - Combat save: Alive, provides +1 NPC ally + information advantage.
   - Persuasion partial: Procession weakened, but Fen still marches (compulsion partly broken).
   - Sleight of Hand grab: Alive, –1 clock, information advantage.

4. **Daxx opportunity:** High Stealth (+15) means he can potentially reach the Red Wizard unseen before Round 1 ends. Reward creative scouting.

5. **Axar duel:** Counterspell will be used. Make the ritual-interruption attempt dramatic.

## Troubleshooting

**Party kills the Red Wizard in Round 1 but doesn't prevent the other 4 from ritualizing:**
- The portal does NOT collapse. Clock remains 0 (no change). They got a quick win but didn't break the ritual completely.

**Party spends >4 rounds on the single Red Wizard:**
- Clock +1 (delay penalty). The encounter was not supposed to take this long.

**Party fully stops the Procession via combat:**
- Clock becomes 0 (–1 from stop + 1 from combat length, assuming >2 rounds). They break even but have Fen + prisoners alive.

**Daxx recognizes Fen but the party doesn't immediately help:**
- Fen marches past, resigned. He can still be recalled later if Daxx uses the procession as a distraction. Give them opportunity but not a guaranteed save.
