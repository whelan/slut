# Battlemap Prompts – Temple of Tiamat

Alle prompts er Foundry-ready, topdown, no grid, no text, no labels.
Standard opløsning: 4096×4096 eller 6000×6000.

---

## Niveau 1 – The Maw

### Primær prompt

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap, top-down view, Temple of Tiamat ground level interior, 
vast bone-paved plaza, five massive staircases leading upward carved from black volcanic 
stone, glowing ritual channels cut into the floor flowing with five colors simultaneously 
(red, green, blue, black, white), towering dragonbone arches 15 meters high forming 
cathedral-like structure, cult barricades made of stacked dragon skulls and bones, 
ash and embers drifting through the air, five-colored ritual energy light casting 
colored shadows everywhere, cult soldiers positioned near barricades, eerie atmospheric 
lighting from below the ritual channels, no grid, no labels, no text, no UI, 
ultra detailed, high resolution, 4096x4096
```

### Alternativ: Infiltration-variant (mere tåget/mørk)

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat entry level, bone-paved floor 
with glowing ritual channels in five colors, massive dragonbone arch gate, heavy shadow 
and smoke obscuring patrol positions, cult figures barely visible through ritual haze, 
atmospheric noir lighting, volcanic stone architecture, five colored light sources 
bleeding through cracks in the structure, no grid, no text, no labels, 
ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Taktiske noter til Foundry

- Map size: 30×40 squares (150×200 fod)
- Ritual channels: markér som hazard terrain (rød outline)
- Knoglebarrikader: placér som walls/obstacles
- Fem trapper: markér som separate zones med "stairs up" tokens

## Niveau 2 – The Fivefold Sanctum

### Primær prompt

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat middle level, five ritual platforms 
radiating outward from a central hub like a five-pointed star, connected by narrow stone 
bridges with no railings and a deadly drop below, each platform glows in a different color:

- BLACK platform: dark acid pool with dragon bones half-submerged, dripping ceiling, 
  black water with iridescent shimmer
- BLUE platform: tall crystal spire crackling with lightning arcs, electric blue glow, 
  crystal formations on floor
- GREEN platform: dense green fog obscuring the surface, faint illusion of forest visible 
  through the mist
- RED platform: circular altar with open flame, glowing magma visible through iron grates 
  in floor, red ember light
- WHITE platform: covered in thick ice and frost, enormous fossilized dragon claw 
  rising from center, deathly still

Central hub: black volcanic stone, five-colored energy streams flowing through channels 
to each platform, stairs visible going up and down, no railings on bridges, 
atmospheric colored lighting from each platform affecting adjacent spaces, 
no grid, no text, no labels, ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Alternativ: "Under angreb"-variant (kaotisk)

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat fivefold sanctum under attack, 
five ritual platforms on bridges over void, BLACK platform: acid splashing from combat, 
BLUE platform: lightning arcing wildly from damaged crystal, GREEN platform: mist 
dispersing showing broken green crystal, RED platform: altar flames extinguished with 
smoke rising, WHITE platform: ice cracking and fragmenting, central hub showing 
signs of battle, five colored energy streams destabilizing, dynamic dramatic lighting, 
no grid, no text, no labels, ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Taktiske noter til Foundry

- Map size: 40×40 squares (200×200 fod)
- Hub: central 6×6 square zone
- Broer: 2 squares brede, markér som narrow passage
- Faldzone: markér udenfor broer som instant death / 8d6 fald
- Hvert platforms hazard: separat lys-farve i Foundry's lighting

## Niveau 3 – The Crown

### Primær prompt

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat ritual apex, circular open-air 
platform 60 feet diameter atop a volcanic temple, black volcanic stone, center of a 
swirling vortex of ash and clouds visible above, massive portal arch at far end 
pulsing deep hellish red with Avernus visible through it, five dragon-head openings 
in the temple wall equally spaced around the circular platform each radiating their 
chromatic color (acid-black, lightning-blue, poison-green, fire-red, frost-white), 
dragon skull pillars providing half cover, ritual energy streams converging at platform 
center from below, dark sky with ash swirling, no railings at platform edge, 
atmospheric dramatic lighting from portal and five dragon head apertures, 
no grid, no text, no labels, ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Alternativ: "Tiamat presser igennem"-variant

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat crown platform in crisis, 
circular ritual apex, massive portal ripping wider with five draconic head shapes 
pressing through from Avernus, each head in different chromatic color partially 
manifested, hellfire and plane-bleed energy flooding the platform, ritual channels 
overloading with five-color energy, dragon skull pillars cracked from energy surge, 
platform edge crumbling, dramatic catastrophic lighting with five color energies 
mixing chaotically, no grid, no text, no labels, ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Alternativ: "Templets kollaps"-variant (flugt)

```
Dark fantasy D&D battlemap top-down, Temple of Tiamat collapsing, circular crown 
platform with massive structural cracks, portal sealing and collapsing in on itself, 
falling stone debris, dust clouds billowing from cracks, platform edge breaking away, 
emergency lighting from dying ritual channels, chaotic destruction, narrow escape routes 
visible at platform edge, no grid, no text, no labels, ultra detailed, 4096x4096
```

### Taktiske noter til Foundry

- Map size: 25×25 squares (125×125 fod) – rund platform
- Platform: cirkulær form, brug Foundry's polygon walls
- Portal zone: 4×4 squares, markér som hazard ved clock 4+
- Dragon head openings: fem punkter symmetrisk, markér som special zones
- Dragon skull pillars: 6 styk, 2×2 squares each, solid walls
