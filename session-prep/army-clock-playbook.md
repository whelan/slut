# Army Clock Playbook – Temple of Tiamat

**Party:** Axar (Wizard/Counterspell), Daxx (Ranger/infiltration), Frygtløs (Barbarian/strength), Twilight (Bard/social)

---

## d8 Frontline Events (party-tuned)

| d8 | Event | PC Hook | Army Clock Effect |
|:---:|---|---|---|
| **1** | Dwarven sapper captain pinned under rubble. Can be lifted by Frygtløs (STR 23). Needs 1 action + no damage taken. | Frygtløs | –1 if successful, +1 if ignored |

| **3** | Metallic dragon ally (Elia) surrounded by two blue drakes. Twilight can sing a *Suggestion* across the 400 feet of battlefield (Bard social range, Performance DC 16) to redirect one drake's focus. Elia breaks free for –1. | Twilight | –1 if Suggestion succeeds, +1 if not attempted |
| **4** | Emerald Enclave treants ablaze. Cult mage visible on a siege tower. Axar can *Counterspell* the mage's next fire spell (or *Fireball*) to save the treants. Action economy, no other benefit. | Axar | No clock change, but Enclave morale +1 (narrative) |

**When to roll:** Between major encounters (after L1, after L2). Or use as atmospheric fillers during the temple descent. Do not feel obligated to use all eight.

## 3 Named NPC Mini-Scenes

### Scene 1: Ulder Ravengard (Flaming Fist Commander)

**Trigger:** After party helps the army (event 1, 2, or 5).

> Ulder ser jer komme tilbage. Hans ansigt er sortet af sod, hans hjælm knust på en side. Han nikker kortvarigt — ikke tak, ikke berømmelse.
>
> "De drager tæller. Hver ti minutter, jeg mister en dusin folk. I såede tid. Gør det værd."

**Mechanical:** Army clock –1 (already resolved by the event). Ravengard signals his officers to hold the position for 10 minutes. Party is not pursued into the temple entrance.

---

### Scene 2: Remallia Haventree (Harper Liaison)

**Trigger:** Party encounters Harper courier (event 7) or reaches temple entrance wounded.

> Remallia træder ud af røgen med den samme kolde kontrol. Hun ser jer vurderende, ikke venligt.
>
> "Leosin betalte for den rute. Seks andre med ham. Hvis I bruger den og fejler, blev det til intet. Du ved det?"

**Mechanical:** If party saved the courier, Remallia gives them the secret passage **to Level 2 (bypass Level 1 entirely)** — costs 2 rounds of climbing, avoids 1 major encounter. **Consequence:** Army clock +0 (party gains time), ritual clock +1 (they skip exposure content, but the ritual is uninterrupted).

If party ignored the courier: Remallia hands them a **vial of Thayan poison antidote** (one use) and a hard look. No clock change.

---

### Scene 3: Ontharr Frume (Order of the Gauntlet)

**Trigger:** After party holds a flank with Frygtløs (event 5) or when Frygtløs is bloodied/down.

> Ontharr kommer op langs siden, blodig og skraldende med armor. Han griber Frygtløs ved skulderen.
>
> "Du holdt. Din slags holder altid, når mændene ikke kan. Tag det med dig derind: orden og styrke betyder noget. Selv når alt brænder."

**Mechanical:** Frygtløs gains **Inspiration (d8)** — usable once during the temple finale. Ontharr's final line: "Slå først. Spørg efter." (Hit first. Ask later.) — subtle callback to Naergoth's death. No army clock change.

## Army ↔ Ritual Clock Coupling

### How Army-Clock events force Ritual-Clock ticks

| Army Scenario | Ritual Consequence |
|---|---|
| Army clock advances to **4** (Dwarves about to open passage) | Ritual clock +1 (Severin senses threat, accelerates ritual) |
| Army clock advances to **6** (Cult seals caldera) | Ritual clock +1 (Portal stabilizes, harder to close later) |
| Army clock reaches **8** (Ritual cannot be attacked from outside) | Ritual clock +2 (Cult redirects all defenders to Level 3, Severin becomes confident) |

### When PCs can intervene WITHOUT costing ritual clock

| PC Action | Ritual Cost | Army Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| Daxx infiltrates and kills a wyvern rider | 0 | Army clock –2 |
| Twilight *Suggestion* redirects a drake | 0 | Army clock –1 |
| Axar *Counterspell* a fire spell on treants | 0 | Morale +1, no clock change |
| Frygtløs holds a flank (3 rounds max) | 0 per round | Army clock –1 per 2 rounds |
| Party kills Abishai (event 6) | 0 | Army clock –1 per round they fight (vs waiting 4 rounds) |

**Key rule:** Actions that leverage PC specialization without stopping the temple ascent do not tick the ritual clock.

### When helping the army DOES cost ritual time

| Action | Ritual Cost | Army Benefit |
|---|---|---|
| Save the Harper courier (full stabilize + Healing Word) | +1 | Army clock –1 + secret passage access |
| Long combat to defend a section of the line | +1 | Army clock –2 |
| Return to ground level to help rebuild the barricade | +3 | Army clock –3 (Cult forces reset) |

## Quick Reference – Managing the War

**Before session:** Decide starting Army clock. Typical: 2–4.

**During Level 1:** Frontline event every 2 encounters. Roll d8 or choose. Let players know they can *hear* explosions and roars.

**During Level 2:** One Frontline event mid-level. Emphasize the foci-sabotage reducing environmental hazards = army advantage.

**Level 3 push:** No random events. Army clock is locked based on what happened. Use it narratively: if Army is at 6+, allied soldiers are panicking outside; if Army is at 2–3, Ravengard's line holds and buys time.

**Final scene:** If Ritual clock hits 8, Army clock becomes irrelevant — the portal is fully open and outside defenders cannot help. If Ritual clock stays at 4–7, Army events happening outside are heard as rumbling. Narrative only, no mechanical coupling.

## Sample Play Sequence

**Starting clocks:** Ritual 0 (party bought time), Army 2.

**L1, encounter 2:** Roll event 5 (Flank collapse). Frygtløs holds gap 3 rounds. Army clock –1 → **1**. Ritual clock +0.

**L2, event (midway):** Roll event 3 (Elia surrounded). Twilight *Suggestion* on blue drake. Army –1 → **0**. Ritual +0. Elia flies back to help next encounter.

**L2, end → L3 transition:** Two foci intact (Black and Red) → Ritual clock +2 → **2**. Army clock still at 0 = alliance holds strong.

**L3, round 1:** Party wins initiative. Ritual clock still at 2. Severin sees the party is fast. Ontharr's line outside holds and buys 5 more minutes.
