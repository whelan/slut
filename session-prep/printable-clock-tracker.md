### Clock Effects Quick Reference

| Clock | Effect | Narrative Feel |
|---|---|---|
| **0–1** | Portal swirls, faint colors visible | Ominous but controlled |
| **2** | Chromatic light beams flare | Ritual accelerates |
| **3** | First head visible; Tiamat's voice heard | Danger increases |
| **4** | Head partway through; 15-ft damage zone | Environmental hazard active |
| **5** | Severin becomes Champion of Tiamat | Boss is supercharged |
| **6** | Head can attack independently | Multiple threats |
| **7** | Severin minimum 3 HP (cannot die yet) | Race against time |
| **8** | **TIAMAT FULLY MANIFESTS** | Campaign failure state |

**Usage Notes:**

- **Print in color** if possible (clock at top for easy visibility)
- **Laminate** for durability (table spills happen)
- **Place on DM screen** or within player line of sight (clock should not be secret)
- **Update every round** by circling the current clock value
- **Army Clock** is optional if you want to narrate frontline battles without mechanical tracking
- **Severin Checklist** is for DM reference; fold it or tuck it behind screen
