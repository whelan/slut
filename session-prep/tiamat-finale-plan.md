### Three Clue Rule – the Naergoth gap

The party missed 6 exposition points by killing Naergoth before he could speak. Per dndtale's Three Clue Rule (`.agents/skills/dndtale/modules/encounter-design.md`), any conclusion the players need to reach requires **at least three independent paths**. Without these alternates, the finale plays under-informed and feels arbitrary.

| Lost exposition | 3 alternate paths in the temple |
|---|---|
| Ritual already underway | (a) Stage 1 Tiamat-presence at the gate, (b) Axar Arcana DC 13 on the Level 1 ritual channels, (c) Severin's pre-combat monologue |
| Severin is on Level 3 | (a) Captured cultist interrogation on Level 1, (b) ritual energy beam visibly traveling UP, (c) the cult-leader portrait gallery (Prompt 11) |
| Five-foci system | (a) Visible from the hub on Level 2, (b) Axar Arcana auto-recognition (epic-character-moments.md Moment 1), (c) bound prisoner who saw Severin assemble it |

| Dracolich vs Tiamat-fanatic cult split | (a) Captured cultist confession, (b) Naergoth's portrait + History (Prompt 11), (c) a dying old cultist's last words on Level 1 |

## The Three Approaches – Comparison

| Approach | Best for | Starts at | PC fit | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Land (Front Line)** | Frygtløs moments, army politics, Ontharr/Ravengard RP | Level 1 main entrance | Frygtløs, Twilight | Slowest – clock pressure, multiple frontline events |
| **Water (Underground)** | Mystery, Axar ritual-disruption, Ghost Dragon RP | Level 1 back-entrance, behind defenders (advantage on initiative round 1) | Axar (Ritual Water Chamber), Daxx (scout) | Acid exposure, navigation, slowest combat |
| **Air (Dogfight)** | Spectacle, speed, skip Level 1 entirely | Level 2 or 3 directly | Daxx, Frygtløs (Winged Boots), Twilight (Sleet Storm) | High fall risk, requires flight resources |

### Recommendation for this party: hybrid

**Land approach** as cinematic opener (army politics, Ontharr / Ravengard scenes, Frygtløs Bone Gate moment outside) → **Lava Tube via Harpers map** into the **Black Chapel** (Level 1 back-entry, per Rise of Tiamat raw).

Rationale:

- Frygtløs gets a Bone Gate moment *outside* the temple (the Sappers scene from `well-of-dragons/tilgange.md`). That gives him constructive physical agency to balance the violent opener at Naergoth's gate.
- Daxx scouts the Lava Tube (Survival/Stealth – both expertise-or-advantage for him).
- Axar gets the Ritual Water Chamber disruption window (–1 ritual clock if Arcana DC 17 holds for 5 rounds).
- Twilight gets The Procession encounter inside (Persuasion DC 15 → 1d6 cultists turn).
- Entry advantage: behind primary defenders = advantage on first initiative.

**Target ritual clock at Level 3 arrival:** ≤4. If higher, Severin becomes Champion of Tiamat or unkillable, and the fight gets very long.

## Phase A – Caldera & Entry

**dndtale framing:** Phase A is the **Cold Open** + opening **Rising Action** (`modules/session-pacing.md`). Drop the players into sensation and decision, not summary. Every boxed text follows `modules/creative-voice.md`: lead with atmosphere, three senses minimum, present tense, end on an implicit question.

### Beat list

1. **Approach decision** – land/water/air or hybrid. Tie to canonical state.
2. **Army front** – Ontharr or Ravengard meets the party. Hær-clock starts ticking.
3. **Frontline event** (1d8 roll or DM-chosen). Frygtløs holds something, Daxx scouts something, Twilight rallies something.
4. **Entry** – Gate of Bone or Lava Tube (Black Chapel back-entry).
5. **The Procession** – moral choice; lands either before or after gate encounter.
6. **Transition to Level 2** – breathing room, 30 seconds for the players to plan, potions, coordinate.

## How to Use This Plan

### Recommended workflow

1. **Lock canonical state.** Fill in the "Open questions" checkboxes above. Calculate starting ritual clock.
2. **Decide approach.** Talk to the party out-of-character (or let them decide in-character at Council of Waterdeep). Lock the approach before prep.
3. **Run prompts in pre-session prep.** Order: 1 → 2 → 3 (or 4) → 5 → 6 → 7 → 8 → 9 → 10. Bridge prompts 11–12 if you want the Reloaded layer and the epilogue arcs.
4. **Generate art with Prompt 13.** Run `dndig` against the output files. Print or display at the table.
5. **Run Prompt 14 (quality + consistency pass)** before the session. Fix hard inconsistencies, note soft issues, ignore confirmed strengths.
6. **Bring outputs to the table.** Print boxed texts. Have the clock cheat-sheet visible. Have Severin's pre-combat updated line ready.
7. **After the session: edit this file.** Note what actually happened — becomes canonical state for the next session. Re-run `checklists/consistency-checklist.md` mentally as you write.

### Example iteration (use this format when asking the assistant)

**Good:**

> Kør prompt 3 (Level 1 walkthrough). Antagelser: party valgte hybrid (Land
> til Lava Tube via Harpers-kort). Starting ritual clock = 2. Hær-clock = 0.
> Twilight har Anstruth Harp aktiv. Frygtløs er fully rested. Adjust the
> Procession beat so Fen is recognized by Daxx specifically (Changeling
> insight on a scared kid). Output i samme format som prompt 3 forventer.

**Less good:**

> Lav Level 1 prep.

### Iteration after the session

After each phase, edit this file:

- Tick off the "Open questions" checkboxes that resolved.
- Update the canonical state with what actually happened (which foci sabotaged, which Abishai fell, did Frygtløs choose to spare Fen, etc.).
- Move resolved prompts to a `# Run log` section at the bottom, with a one-line note ("Prompt 5 run on 2026-05-14, used Green→Red→Black→Skip-White→Blue").

### When to abandon this plan

If the party does something completely off-script (e.g. negotiates with Severin and walks away), put this file aside and improvise. The plan is scaffolding — the campaign is theirs.
