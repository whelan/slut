# Scene 4b — The Plaza Sacrifice (If Party Sneaks to Plaza Edge)

**Trigger:** Party successfully sneaks past tunnel guards using Stealth DC 16 (or higher), reaches the plaza edge without major combat noise. They emerge as the sacrifice ritual is completing.

**Canonical timing:** Severin is on Level 3 preparing the final ritual. The foci are being awakened. At this moment, the gathering of cultists on the plaza is reaching its crescendo.

---

## The Moment They Reach the Edge

### Boxed Text (Dansk)

> I kommer ud på plazaen, og verden ændrer sig.
>
> Det er stort. Ikke blot stort — det strækker sig så langt som I kan se i begge retninger, en åben åbning i bjerget, hvor asken falder som hvidt kød fra en eller anden himmelske sårrelse. Over jer kredser drager — fem, måske seks, blot konturerne synlige mod det mørke lys — og hver gang de kredser, falder deres skygger over jer som om verden selv tæller ned.
>
> Og i det hele tagen — i midte af denne øde, øde plads — står der mennesker.
>
> Der er måske tyve af dem. Eller tredive. De er klædt i rødt, eller hvidt, eller sorte robes der flyder som røg. De står med håndene løftet eller knyttede eller spredt ud til siderne, og de chanter. Ikke som en enhed — som fem grupper, hver gruppe med deres egen stemme, men alle i samme hastighed, som om deres hjerner er forbundet af noget der ligger længere ned end tanke.
>
> Foran dem — på en sten-altaret der stikker op fra asken som en tand — står fem mennesker der burde være løbet væk.
>
> De er bundet. Deres øjne er lukket eller stirrer. En mand i rødsilke — ikke Severin, ikke sådan, men nær ham — løfter en dolk, og det gør han langsomt nok til at I kan høre det skære gennem luften.
>
> Og så falder den.

Give them **names and faces** so the death means something:

**Alternative if Fen WAS saved:** The fifth prisoner is **a nameless cultist** who broke from mind control and tried to escape. His sacrifice is punishment.

## The Ritual Master

**Magus Thezzar** (Red Wizard lieutenant, on Rath Modar's command) performs the ritual on the plaza. Rath Modar remains in the temple (Red Chapel, Level 2) coordinating the entire ritual from above via magical resonance. Thezzar is:

- Middle-aged human male, scarred face (acid burns from a ritual gone wrong)
- Speaks with precise, cold authority — Rath's voice transmitted through him
- Wears crimson robes with black trim; carries the ritual dagger (obsidian blade)
- AC 15 (Mage Armor), HP 40, Spells: *Fireball*, *Hold Person*, *Misty Step*

**Thezzar's role:** He is the executioner, not the architect. He follows Rath Modar's mental commands (they are linked via ritual bond). If killed, the ritual's coordination breaks momentarily.

**Rath Modar's presence:** The party hears his voice echoing from the temple above:
- *"The blood must flow in sequence, Thezzar."*
- *"Do not rush the fifth. The Queen demands precision."*

This establishes Rath as the true power while keeping him available for the temple finale.

## What the Party Witnesses (Round by Round)

### Round 1 — Recognition

> I ser fyrsten af jer kender. Eller måske I ser ham ikke — det er for langt, skyggerne er for mørk — men I ser at nogen der er mennesker bliver til ikke-mennesker. I ser at ritualet ikke længere er abstrakt ord. Det er blod i asken. Det er stilhed efter et menneskes sidste vejr.

**Mechanical:** 
- Any PC with a personal connection to one of the prisoners (Harper liaison, Baldur's Gate city contact) must make a WIS save DC 15 or be Frightened until the end of their next turn.
- Clock advances +1 (first death).

### Round 2 — Understanding

> Den anden bliver dræbt. Og så den tredje. Og så den fjerde. Ikke i slag — I ville kunne modstå det hvis det var kamp. Det er blot dødsfald, efterhånden, som om døden selv er et ritual, en ændring af tilstande, og ingen af dem ville komme ud af det andet sted.
>
> Templet over jer hviner højere.

**Mechanical:**
- Clock advances +1 per death (so after 4 deaths, clock is +4).
- If Fen is in the circle: Daxx must make a WIS save DC 16 or be Stunned 1 round (cannot move or take actions; helpless as he watches). Frygtløs might react with rage or violence (Barbarian Rage triggered automatically if he hasn't used it).

### Round 3 — The Final Death

The fifth prisoner is brought forward. If it's **Fen**, he is still chanting the ritual words, not understanding they are his own death warrant.

> Den sidste — manden eller drengen eller hvem han var før ritualet havde hævn på ham — løftes til alteret. Og Rath Modar råber noget på et språk I ikke forstår, men hvis betydning I forstår perfekt.
>
> Drengens øjne åbner sig for første gang.
>
> Han ser jer.
>
> Og så bliver der ikke mere syn.

**Mechanical:**
- Clock advances +1 (now +5 or higher depending on how many deaths occurred).
- If Daxx was stunned, he's free on his next turn and can react.
- All party members must make WIS save DC 16 or take 1 level of Exhaustion (the horror presses down).

## The Aftermath — Cultist Reaction

**The cultists DO NOT immediately see the party.** They are deep in the ritual trance. But if the party makes ANY sound or movement:
- Drake Perception DC 12: They notice.
- Cultists will attempt to **protect the ritual** by converging on the party.
- Rath Modar will **not** leave the altar — he must complete the final channeling.

### If Party Attempts Rescue (Before All Deaths)

- **DC 16 Acrobatics check** to reach the altar before Thezzar can strike the next prisoner.
- **Contested Perception vs. Party Stealth** if they try to sneak closer without alerting cultists.
- **If successful:** One prisoner can be unbound and **escapes toward the temple entrance** (or back toward the tunnel).
- **Clock impact:** Ritual disrupted; –1 clock (cancels 1 death's clock advancement).
- **Combat risk:** Cultists (Dragonfang, Dragonwing, Red Wizard) + **Thezzar** engage the party. A hard fight with 15+ enemies.
- **Rath Modar's response:** His voice booms from the temple: *"KILL THEM, THEZZAR! THE QUEEN'S HUNGER CANNOT WAIT!"* Thezzar uses his full spell arsenal (Fireball, Hold Person) to defend the ritual.

### If Party Cannot Intervene

- All five die. Clock +5 (worst-case scenario from this scene).
- **Emotional beat:** Party must watch helplessly.
- **Character moment:** Especially for Daxx (if Fen dies) and Frygtløs (if he recognizes a martyr).

## Axar's Arcana Opportunity

As the ritual completes, **Axar makes an Arcana check DC 16:**

- **Success:** He understands the ritual geometry. Five deaths → five foci being awakened simultaneously. The party now knows: if they sabotage the foci on Level 2, they can partially undo this work. Axar also realizes that **Rath Modar is channeling this ritual from the temple above** — the voice commanding Thezzar is not just coordination, it's a direct magical link. This suggests Rath is on Level 2 (Red Chapel), not on Level 3 with Severin. Ritual knowledge gained: –1 to clock at Level 2 Foci encounter (one sabotage is "free").
- **Failure:** He sees the power, but not the structure. The ritual is complete. He does not realize Rath Modar's location.

## DM Notes

- **Emotional weight:** Don't rush this scene. Let players absorb the horror. Pause after each death.
- **Rath Modar's voice:** His commands from the temple reinforce that he's the true architect. The party hears him, not Thezzar's thoughts. This foreshadows that Rath is on Level 2 (Red Chapel) and NOT with Severin on Level 3.
- **Thezzar is expendable:** He's a lieutenant, not a boss. His role is to execute Rath's will. If killed, cultists panic momentarily, but the ritual doesn't stop — Rath can command another wizard.
- **Fen's death (if applicable):** This is the climax of the Frygtløs parallel (Naergoth → Fen choice). Let it land.
- **Daxx's moment:** If Fen dies, Daxx may attempt a solo rescue in the next round — allow it, but the DC checks are brutal (DC 18+).
- **The silence after:** When the fifth prisoner falls, describe the moment where the ritual completes. The cultists' chanting stops. The wind on the plaza picks up. The temple goes quiet, waiting for Level 3 to begin. Rath's voice from the temple: *"It is done. The Queen's hunger is sated. For now."*
